<h1>IIT Bombay English-Hindi Corpus</h1></br>

<h2>About</h2></br>
The IIT Bombay English-Hindi Parallel corpus contains parallel corpus for  English-Hindi as well as monolingual Hindi corpus collected from a variety of existing sources and corpora developed at the Center for Indian Language Technology, IIT Bombay over the years. This page describes the corpus. This corpus has been used at the  <a href="http://lotus.kuee.kyoto-u.ac.jp/WAT/index.html">Workshop on Asian Language Translation Shared Task</a> since 2016 the Hindi-to-English and English-to-Hindi languages pairs and as a pivot language pair for the Hindi-to-Japanese and Japanese-to-Hindi language pairs. </br>
